Verb to be

O verbo to be possui diferentes formas. Ele atua como os verbos ser/estar.
A seguir temos as estruturas do verbo to be:

Positive form I am happy.
Subject + to be + complement
Negative form I am not happy.
Subject + to be not + complement
Question form Am I happy.
To be + subject + complement
